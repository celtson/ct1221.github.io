The Prince Warriors

The Prince Warriors is one of the first websites I knocked out that started from the beginning stages. I had the opportunity to flesh out wireframes based off of a small amount of information given. I later turned the wireframes into a design and the design into a functional WordPress site. The website focuses on the author Priscilla Shirer's new book series, The Prince Warriors. It elegantly lays out the books on the home page and dives deeper into each book's details as the user flows through the site.

Levels of Biblical Learning

The Levels of Biblical Learning was a fun one to work on considering I did not have to bother with a Content Management system. Brandon Woodall, my remarkable co-worker designed the site, after which, I produced the HTML, CSS, and jQuery to make his design come to life. This site digitally lays out biblical concepts presented through six different age ranges, from infancy through the preteen years.
